About Us

Extension Education and Communication Management is a key discipline in Community Science at the College of Community Science, Dr. Rajendra Prasad Central Agricultural University. Since the college's establishment, courses related to this discipline have been integrated into the undergraduate curriculum.The department is committed to training students in Information and Communication techniques and technologies, with the goal of effectively transferring relevant technologies and practices to rural families. This training ensures the adoption of improved technologies for better family living and enhances students' professional opportunities.A core aspect of our department is to ensure that students understand the unique challenges faced by rural families, especially in terms of livelihoods, nutrition, health, and sustainable resource management. We work to empower students to communicate effectively, enabling them to transfer appropriate home science and agricultural technologies to improve the quality of life in rural households.
